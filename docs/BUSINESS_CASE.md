# Business Case & Market Analysis

## 1. Executive Summary

**gstGPT** addresses the critical pain point of GST compliance for the **63 million MSMEs** in India. By leveraging Generative AI, we automate the complex, repetitive, and error-prone process of tax filing, transforming it from a monthly burden into a seamless background operation.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

### The "Compliance Tax" on Business
For Indian businesses, GST is not just a financial tax; it's a "cognitive tax."
- **Complexity**: The GST law is dynamic, with over **1,000+ notifications/amendments** since inception.
- **Frequency**: A typical business files **24-36 returns per year** (GSTR-1, 3B, 9).
- **Cost of Error**: A single mismatch in Input Tax Credit (ITC) can lead to significant working capital blockage and penalties.
- **Dependency**: Businesses are heavily dependent on Chartered Accountants (CAs), leading to bottlenecks during filing dates.

---

## 3. Solution: gstGPT

gstGPT is an **AI-First Tax Agent** that acts as a 24/7 virtual consultant.

| Feature | Benefit |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Instant Answers** | No need to Google or call a CA for basic queries. |
| **Auto-Drafting** | Reduces manual data entry errors by 90%. |
| **Real-Time Insights** | "What is my liability?" is answered instantly, not after month-end closing. |
| **Proactive Alerts** | "You have 2 days to file GSTR-3B" — prevents penalties. |

## 4. Market Analysis (India)

### Serviceable Available Market (SAM)
- **Focus**: Tech-savvy MSMEs and Startups (Tier 1 & 2 Cities).
- **Estimate**: ~4 Million businesses.

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
- **Goal**: Capture 1% market share in Year 1.

## 5. Customer Personas & Interviews

### Persona A: "Rajesh, the Trader"
*Owner of a Hardware Store in Pune. Turnover: ₹2 Cr.*

> **Interview Insight**: "I spend 3 days every month just collating bills for my CA. If I miss a bill, I lose credit. I don't understand the portal; I just pay what my CA tells me."

**Pain Point**: Time consumption, lack of visibility.
**gstGPT Value**: Automates bill collation, provides instant visibility into liability.

### Persona B: "Sneha, the CA"
*Practicing Chartered Accountant in Mumbai.*

> **Interview Insight**: "During filing week, my staff works 14 hours a day. We spend too much time on data entry and basic queries from clients. I want to focus on advisory, not data entry."

**Pain Point**: Low-value repetitive work, scalability bottleneck.
**gstGPT Value**: Acts as a "Junior Assistant" for the CA, handling routine queries and drafts.
